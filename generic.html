DVD RENTAL

What I did

In this project, I analysed data gotten from a
dvd rental company. The company has six categories
of films:

  • Animation
  • Music
  • Family
  • Children
  • Classics
  • Comedy

Postgres was used in analysing the and comparing data
in csvformat.
I was able to analyse various statistics such as:

  • The highest paying customer for a particular month or year.
  • The film category with the most rental order.
  • The film category with the highest number of movies.

You can check out the project
by clicking DVD RENTAL.
