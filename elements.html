BIKESHARE

What I did

In this bike share project, I analysed data gotten from a
bikesharing company across three major cities:
Chicago, New York and Washingtion.

The data from the three cities was in a csv(comma separated values)
format and was loaded into pandas dataframe in python.
I was able to analyse various statistics such as:

  • Most popular month and day for bike riding.
  • Most popular travel time.
  • Most popular stations
  • Total travel distance covered, and so on.

You can check out the project on my git repository
by clicking bikeshare.
